ABOUT

Joy Tours is a reputable and well-established travel agency based in Sri Lanka. Since its inception in 2014, the company has been providing exceptional travel and tourism services to its clients. In recognition of its outstanding services, Victoria Travels was awarded the Best Travel Agency in Sri Lanka at the International Travel Awards in 2022. With over 10 years of experience in the travel management industry, Joy Tours has earned a strong reputation for delivering 5-star quality services and achieving one of the top three highest sales among travel companies in Sri Lanka. We have established a wide network and strong partnerships with 18 branches across the country. As an IATA accredited travel agency, Joy Tours offers a wide range of services. These include flight bookings with over 400 airlines worldwide, accommodation in more than 500,000 hotels and similar properties, visa assistance, travel insurance, cruise bookings, train bookings, ground logistics including airport transfers, inbound and outbound tours, and MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conventions/Conferences, and Exhibitions) travel services. Reasons to choose Joy Tours include our wide network of branches across Sri Lanka, 24/7 responsive support through a dedicated contact center, aftersales team for support and feedback, multilingual employees, global reach through partnerships, bespoke luxury services for VIP clients, personalized account management, and the ability to control business travel budgets. We offer you detailed information and local tips about famous places and unknown areas. Big cities, small villages, natural areas are listed. We help you to balance your journey. We provide you local trips so you can go off the beaten track, meet people and get to know their daily life.
